About Chad Tao

Born on December 5th, 1988 in the People's Republic of China, Chad Tao was given the name of "Chen Tao" which those closest to him call him by. He moved to the United States at the tender age of 12, not speaking a word of English at first. But despite those great odds against him succeeding, Chad was able to achieve mediocrity in his studies and career. He is now married to his beautiful wife Fay and they have two daughters together. Chad is a gourmet connoisseur and enjoys pumping iron at the gym once or twice every few months.
